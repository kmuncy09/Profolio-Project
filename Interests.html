Interests

Piano

I have been playing piano since 3rd grade. I take weekly lessons from Karla Carlile. Every year, I compete in the Indiana Arts Festival, formally known as Federation, where I have won several trophies.

Cello

I have been playing cello since 5th grade. Most of my extracurriculars surround this instrument. I am a member of the Evansville Philharmonic Youth Orchestra (EPYO). In middle school, I participated in the EVSC Middle School Honors Orchestra. I take cello lessons weekly from Graham Cullen. In school, I am a member of Signature's orchestra. I compete in ISSMA Solo and Ensemble, advancing to state for the past 2 years.

Reading

Ever since my first book, I have loved to read. My favorite genres are realistic and historical fiction. As I have gotten older, dystopian romance has grown on me. Some books and series I like are Divergent, Legend, Boxcar Children, Mad Honey, The War That Saved My Life, and Most Likely.
